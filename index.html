Quicktest.diy – Smart Quiz Creation

Quicktest.diy is a smart, user-friendly quiz creation and assessment platform designed for educators, students, and trainers. The platform simplifies the entire quiz workflow — from creating questions to evaluating results — making assessments faster, engaging, and more efficient.

Smart Platform Assessment Tool UI/UX Design Education
My Role UI/UX & Product Designer
Platforms Web & Mobile Responsive
Live Site Visit Site

The Challenge

Educators and trainers faced several critical challenges that needed an intelligent solution:

  • Creating quizzes was time-consuming and repetitive.
  • Managing legacy question banks lacked proper structure.
  • Manual evaluation of student work consumed excessive effort.
  • Limited analytics made performance tracking difficult.
  • Poor user experience in existing assessment tools.
  • Students lacked instant feedback and meaningful insights.

Opportunity: Create a simple, intuitive, and intelligent quiz platform that streamlines the entire assessment process.

Research & Key Insights

Target Users

School/college educators, corporate trainers, online course creators, and students preparing for exams.

User Needs

Users prioritized speed in quiz creation, auto-evaluation, instant feedback, and clean, clutter-free user flows.

Clean, minimal, workflow-driven experiences reduce friction and significantly enhance platform usability.

Design Process

01.
User Flow Planning

Designed a simplified workflow: Create Quiz → Add Questions → Configure → Publish → Track Results.

02.
Wireframing

Sketched low-fidelity layouts for the question builder and dashboard to ensure clear hierarchy and CTA visibility.

03.
UI Design

Developed a dashboard-style interface with a card-based question builder and clear data visualizations.

Core Features

Smart Quiz Builder

Support for MCQs, True/False, and Short Answer with easy editing, duplication, and management.

Performance Analytics

Visual charts for score tracking, accuracy percentages, and detailed attempt histories per student.

Auto Evaluation

Instant scoring and automated result generation for immediate learner feedback loops.

Responsive Design

Fully optimized interface ensuring a consistent experience across desktop and mobile devices.

Design Highlights

The design system prioritizes clarity and efficiency for a professional assessment environment.

  • Minimal UI: Focused and distraction-free environment for creators and learners.
  • Visual Hierarchy: Strong use of typography and color to guide users through tasks.
  • Data Visualization: Intuitive charts that make complex results instantly readable.
  • Structured Spacing: Consistent use of soft shadows and margins to organize content.

Impact & Results

  • Significant reduction in quiz creation time for educators.
  • Improved teacher productivity through automated grading tools.
  • Enhanced student engagement through instant result availability.
  • Simplified result analysis for high-level performance tracking.
  • Improved usability and task completion rate across the platform.

What I Learned

Workflow Optimization

The importance of reducing clicks and simplifying complex multi-step processes for better UX.

User Balancing

Designing effectively for two distinct user types: the quiz creator and the end learner.

Balancing high functionality with a minimal UI is key to making data-heavy platforms truly readable and intuitive.

Quizzer.ai – Smart Assessment

Quizzer.ai is an AI-powered learning and assessment platform that automatically generates quizzes, evaluates responses, and provides personalized insights to improve knowledge retention and performance.

AI-Powered Adaptive Learning Product Design UI/UX
My Role UI/UX & Product Designer
Focus End-to-end Experience
Live Site Visit Project

The Challenge

Traditional quiz platforms faced limitations that hindered both educators and learners:

  • Manual question creation was time-consuming and repetitive.
  • No personalization based on individual user performance.
  • Limited feedback beyond simple score displays.
  • Lack of adaptive difficulty levels to challenge students appropriately.
  • Weak data insights for educators to track learning gaps.
  • Static and non-engaging overall learning experience.

The Goal: Create a smart, adaptive assessment experience that goes beyond traditional quizzes by using AI to enhance learning outcomes.

Research & Key Insights

Target Users

Online learners, Competitive exam aspirants, EdTech platforms, Course creators, and Educational Institutions.

User Pain Points

Learners wanted quizzes tailored to their weak areas; teachers needed quick assessment creation; students preferred instant, detailed feedback.

Key Opportunity: Design a platform that generates quizzes using AI, adapts difficulty based on performance, and provides personalized learning insights.

Design Process

01.
User Journey Mapping

Designed two major flows: For Educators (Create → AI Generate → Review → Publish) and Learners (Select → Attempt → AI Evaluation → Insights).

02.
Wireframing

Created low & mid-fidelity wireframes for the AI generation screen, adaptive quiz player, and results/insights dashboard focused on AI transparency.

03.
UI Design

Developed a clean, modern AI-focused interface with minimal layouts, strong hierarchy, and progress tracking visuals for a distraction-free experience.

Core Features

AI Quiz Generation

Topic-based structured questions across multiple formats (MCQ, Short Answer, T/F), editable before publishing.

Personalized Insights

Strength/weakness analysis, topic-wise breakdown, and personalized improvement suggestions.

Adaptive Difficulty

Real-time question adjustment based on user accuracy to encourage continuous learning.

Instant Evaluation

Automated scoring coupled with AI-based feedback suggestions for immediate learning loops.

Design Highlights

The visual identity focuses on transparency and intelligence, using soft elements to represent AI-driven guidance.

  • AI-First Visual Identity: Clean, modern interface with soft gradients indicating AI intelligence.
  • Insight-Driven Dashboard: Readable metrics via insight cards and data visualization for clarity.
  • Minimal Distractions: Layout designed to keep the focus on learning content.
  • Responsive Interface: Fully optimized for seamless switching between desktop and mobile devices.
  • Clear CTAs: Structured hierarchy to guide users through the assessment workflow.

Impact & Results

  • Reduced quiz creation effort significantly for educators.
  • Improved learner engagement through interactive and adaptive flows.
  • Increased knowledge retention with personalized feedback loops.
  • Better visibility of performance trends for institutions.
  • Faster feedback cycle, enabling more frequent assessments.

Key Learnings

AI Transparency

Designing for AI transparency builds user trust and encourages them to use automated features more frequently.

Data Presentation

Clean data presentation is critical for learning platforms to ensure users aren't overwhelmed by metrics.

Adaptive systems require clear feedback loops to keep learners motivated and aware of their progress.

Coherence.services – Bridging Gaps

Coherence.services is a transformational platform that connects education, industry, and individuals to foster clarity, meaningful learning, real-world exposure, and collaborative growth. It’s designed to create purposeful educational journeys and professional development pathways that link curiosity with career clarity and impact.

EdTech Bridge Industry Connect Product Architecture Strategic Design
My Role UI/UX & Product Designer
Focus Platform Ecosystem
Live Site Visit Site

The Challenge

Traditional learning and professional development systems often lack meaningful connection between academics and real-world industry needs. This leads to gaps in practical exposure, career misalignment, and a disconnect between education and employment.

  • Lack of meaningful connection between academia and industry.
  • No structured learning journeys with real practical exposure.
  • No clear pathways from curiosity to defined career goals.
  • Lack of inclusive programs for students, professionals, and startups.
  • Disconnected communities that hinder cross-sector collaboration.
  • The frustration of being stuck in theory without real-world application.

Opportunity: Design a unified platform that helps users evolve from curiosity → clarity → capability → impact.

Research & Key Insights

Target Users

Students seeking exposure, Professionals wanting career growth, Startups needing strategy, and Educators seeking relevance.

Key Insights

Users want experiential learning; students need industry-ready programs; professionals need career transition clarity.

Impact-driven education requires a journey that moves from academic curiosity to professional capability.

Design Process

01.
Audience Mapping

Defined distinct journeys for students (curiosity to clarity), professionals (skills and growth), and organizations (strategy and engagement).

02.
UX Architecture

Structured the platform into core pathways: Journeys, Edge, Catalyst, and Blueprint, bolstered by trust indicators and ecosystem highlights.

03.
UI Design

Focused on a welcoming visual hierarchy, intuitive progression, and consistent imagery showing real human connection and transformation.

Core Features & Pathways

Coherence Journeys

Structured learning pathways designed to move users from education to professional clarity.

Coherence Edge

Skill unlocking and performance elevation programs for students and professionals.

Coherence Catalyst

A launchpad for turning innovative ideas into real-world projects and impact.

Coherence Blueprint

Deep insights and strategic roadmaps that drive organizational transformation.

Design Highlights

The visual direction focuses on trust, growth, and professional excellence through a balanced UI.

  • Welcoming Hero Section: Clearly states the purpose of "Building bridges..." to establish immediate mission.
  • Visual Pathway Organization: Services organized beautifully for intuitive user navigation.
  • Trusted Partner Logos: Integrated showcase of partner schools and corporations to build credibility.
  • Clean Journey Layouts: Varied layouts tailored to the unique goals of different user audiences.
  • Engagement Points: Clear 'Get in Touch' prompts strategically placed for program inquiries.

Impact & Outcomes

  • Established clear positioning as the bridge between education and industry.
  • Significantly improved alignment between user expectations and platform offerings.
  • Enhanced navigation flow for multiple personas (Students, Orgs, Professionals).
  • Strong thematic consistency across disparate service categories and pathways.
  • Improved user retention through more cohesive journey-based navigation.

What I Learned

Multi-Persona Design

Learned to design for multiple user types on one platform without losing clarity or direction.

Depth vs. Clarity

How to present deep educational content while maintaining a simple and inviting user interface.

Creating modular design systems is critical to allow platforms to evolve and grow with new service offerings over time.
